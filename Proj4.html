Image Manipulation

November 2018, Project Lead the Way Principles of Engineering

Partner(s): Khushi Gupta, Antonia Leung, Connor Meece

The purpose of this project was to design a prototype Hydrogen-Solar Vehicle that could be scaled up to a full-sized car. Our group tested various configurations of Hydrogen and Solar fuel cells and measured the speed of the vehicle in such configurations. My role in the probject larely concerned the mounting of solar and hydrogen fuel cells, the testing of the vehicle, and the calculations he came along with the vehicle.
